Chunhua Shen

My research interest is Computer Vision.

I am an Adjunct Professor of Data Science and AI at Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University. I was a Senior Lecturer/Associate Professor/Professor at The University of Adelaide from 2011 to 2020. I was awarded an ARC Future Fellowship in 2012. Prior to that I worked at NICTA and Australian National University, Canberra from Oct. 2005 to early 2011.

I did my PhD in computer vision at The University of Adelaide in 2005. I hold a BSc degree from Nanjing University, Department for Intensive Instruction (some of my classmates); and two master degrees (signal processing from Nanjing University, and applied statistics from Australian National University).
